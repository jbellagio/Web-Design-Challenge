Wind Speed


This image shows a trend of cities with higher latitudes having higher wind speeds, on average. As you can see, the only cities with wind speeds greater than 25 mph are cities with a latitude of greater than 40. This data also shows that if the city is between -20 and 0 degrees latitude then it will have the lowest wind speed, on average.
